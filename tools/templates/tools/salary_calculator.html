Methodology: How We Calculate Compensation

Unlike generic salary aggregators that lump "Marketing Managers" together with "Marketing Operations Managers," our calculator is purpose-built for the MarTech industry. We analyze three critical vectors:

  • Role Hierarchy: We distinguish between "Operators" (campaign execution) and "Architects" (system design). Architects typically command a 20-30% premium.
  • Tech Stack Premium: Not all tools pay the same. Enterprise platforms like Salesforce Marketing Cloud and Marketo generally offer higher compensation floors than SMB tools due to the complexity of implementation.
  • Technical Skills: The "Engineer Marketer"—someone who knows SQL, Python, or API integration—is the highest-paid segment in the market today.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are these salaries for remote roles?

Yes, our baseline data is weighted towards Remote and Hybrid roles in the United States technology sector. Adjust downward ~15% for non-tech industries.

Do certifications increase salary?

Yes. Specific certifications like the Marketo Certified Expert (MCE) or Salesforce Marketing Cloud Consultant correlate with a $10k-$15k salary increase for mid-level roles.

Why Email Signatures Matter in HubSpot

Your email signature is often the most overlooked piece of marketing real estate. For HubSpot users, a well-coded signature is crucial because it ensures that your emails (sent via the CRM) look consistent and professional across all devices.

This generator creates clean, HTML-based signatures that are specifically tested for:

  • HubSpot CRM: Paste directly into your profile settings.
  • Gmail & Outlook: Ensures images don't break or look distorted.
  • Mobile Responsiveness: Scales correctly on iPhone and Android mail apps.

How to Install in HubSpot

  1. Generate your signature using the tool above.
  2. Click Copy Signature.
  3. Log in to HubSpot and go to Settings > General > Email > Signature.
  4. Paste the code into the editor and click Save.
